About HeliosX Group

HeliosX Group is a digital health company committed to transforming healthcare by making it more accessible, effective and trustworthy for everyone. Founded in 2013 by Dwayne D'Souza, we began with a vision to simplify healthcare, recognizing that while other industries had embraced convenience and speed, healthcare remained slow and complicated.

We are the world's largest vertically-integrated healthcare network of clinicians and pharmacies, capable of prescribing and delivering medicines globally. Our digital health platform offers the most convenient access to the best prescription treatments for customers living with chronic conditions, empowering them to take control of their health and lead happier, healthier lives.
