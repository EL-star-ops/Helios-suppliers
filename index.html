About HeliosX Group

HeliosX Group is a digital health company committed to transforming healthcare by making it more accessible, effective, and trustworthy for everyone. Founded in 2013 by Dwayne D'Souza, we began with a vision to simplify healthcare, recognizing that while other industries had embraced convenience and speed, healthcare remained slow and complicated.

We are the world's largest vertically-integrated healthcare network of clinicians and pharmacies, capable of prescribing and delivering medicines across the globe. Our digital health platform provides the most convenient access to the best prescription treatments for customers living with chronic conditions.

Our Global Operations

Headquartered in Liverpool Street, London, with offices in London Docklands (our distribution centre) and our US pharmacy in Florida, we service hundreds of thousands of patients across the UK and US. At the heart of each of our brands is an asynchronous telemedicine process removing bottlenecks, increasing access and improving efficiency through technology.

Our Mission: We remove barriers to care by providing seamless access to the medications people need—no trips to the doctor, no waiting in pharmacy lines, and no added complexity. Our approach empowers customers to manage their health privately and conveniently, supported by a team of registered doctors and pharmacists.

CHAS Compliance & Safety Standards

HeliosX Group is committed to the highest health and safety standards. We work exclusively with CHAS-accredited suppliers for all contracting services to ensure comprehensive health and safety compliance across our operations.

🛡️ CHAS Accreditation

All contractors must be CHAS (Contractors Health and Safety Assessment Scheme) accredited. We support suppliers through the accreditation process.

📋 Safety Documentation

Comprehensive health and safety policies, risk assessments, method statements, and compliance with UK health and safety regulations.

🎯 Ongoing Compliance

Regular safety audits, incident reporting systems, and continuous improvement programs to maintain the highest safety standards.

CHAS Partnership: We work with CHAS (Contractors Health and Safety Assessment Scheme) to ensure all our contractors meet rigorous health and safety standards. This pre-qualification system helps us maintain consistent safety excellence across all supplier relationships.

Supplier Requirements & Standards

Supplier Onboarding Process

Our comprehensive onboarding process ensures that all suppliers meet our high standards for quality, compliance, and service excellence.

1

Initial Application

Complete our secure online supplier application form with email verification, providing detailed company information, capabilities, and certifications.

2

Technical Review

Our procurement and technical teams conduct thorough evaluation of your capabilities, compliance status, and alignment with our requirements within 5-7 business days.

3

Compliance Assessment

Comprehensive regulatory compliance verification, quality system audits, facility inspections (where applicable), and reference checks with existing clients.

4

Commercial Negotiation

Discussion of commercial terms, service level agreements, pricing structures, payment terms, and performance metrics for successful partnership.

5

Partnership Activation

Successful suppliers receive comprehensive onboarding materials, partnership agreements, access to supplier portal, and integration with our procurement systems.
