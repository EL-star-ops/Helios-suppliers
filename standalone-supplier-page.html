Supplier Requirements & Standards

To maintain our commitment to excellence and regulatory compliance, we require our suppliers to meet specific standards appropriate to their service category.

🏭 Manufacturing & Production

GMP certification, MHRA manufacturing licenses, validated quality control systems, pharmaceutical manufacturing capabilities, and full traceability systems for all production processes.

🚚 Logistics & Distribution

GDP compliance, cold chain capabilities with validated temperature monitoring, secure distribution networks, emergency supply capabilities, and comprehensive logistics insurance coverage.

⚖️ Regulatory & Compliance

MHRA wholesale dealer authorizations, pharmacovigilance systems, regulatory affairs expertise, audit readiness, and comprehensive understanding of pharmaceutical regulations.

🔒 Data Protection & Security

Full GDPR compliance, advanced cybersecurity measures, patient data protection protocols, ISO 27001 certification preferred, and comprehensive staff security training.

💼 Professional Services

Relevant professional qualifications, healthcare industry experience, professional indemnity insurance, continuous professional development, and proven track record in healthcare sector.

🌐 Technology Solutions

Healthcare technology expertise, data security compliance, scalable infrastructure, API integration capabilities, and experience with regulated healthcare environments.

Supplier Onboarding Process

Our comprehensive onboarding process ensures that all suppliers meet our high standards for quality, compliance, and service excellence.

1

Initial Application

Complete our comprehensive supplier onboarding form with detailed company information, capabilities, certifications, and regulatory compliance status.

2

Technical Review

Our procurement and technical teams conduct thorough evaluation of your capabilities, compliance status, and alignment with our requirements within 5-7 business days.

3

Compliance Assessment

Comprehensive regulatory compliance verification, quality system audits, facility inspections (where applicable), and reference checks with existing clients.

4

Commercial Negotiation

Discussion of commercial terms, service level agreements, pricing structures, payment terms, and performance metrics for successful partnership.

5

Partnership Activation

Successful suppliers receive comprehensive onboarding materials, partnership agreements, access to supplier portal, and integration with our procurement systems.
